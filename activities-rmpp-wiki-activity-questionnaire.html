Formative Activities: Unit 5

This is unit 5 formative activity continuation. A wiki activity questionnaire summary.

The Security Questionnaire is a detailed assessment tool used to gather information about an organization's IT infrastructure, security systems, and compliance readiness. It covers areas such as network setup, system configurations, logging, user authentication, encryption, and incident detection and response.

While the questions are technically relevant and thorough, many are overly complex or open-ended, which may limit clarity and consistency in responses. Some questions use jargon or compound formats that are difficult to answer accurately. Additionally, the questionnaire lacks structure for easy analysis or scoring and overlooks user behavior and training aspects of cybersecurity.

To improve the questionnaire, questions should be simplified, grouped logically, and offered with clearer formats like multiple choice or dropdowns. Adding brief explanations, a scoring system, and a user-awareness section would significantly enhance its effectiveness and usability.

References:

  • Ashrafi Papia, S.A., 2016. Cyber Security Questionnaire. [online] Scribd. Available at: https://www.scribd.com/document/314237255/Cyber-Security-Questionnaire [Accessed 24 Apr. 2025].
