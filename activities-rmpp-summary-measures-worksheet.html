Formative Activities: Unit 5

This is Unit 7 Formative Activity. Summary Measures Worksheet.

Exercise 6.1 - the task is:

  1. Calculate sample size, sample mean, and sample standard deviation for Diet B (not Diet A this time).
  2. Place your results into cells F23 to F25.
  3. Format the mean and standard deviation to three decimal places.
  4. Interpret the results briefly, comparing Diet A and Diet B.

Interpretation:

The sample size for Diet B was 50 individuals. The sample mean weight loss for Diet B was 3.710 kg, with a sample standard deviation of 2.769 kg. Compared to Diet A, where the mean weight loss was 5.341 kg and the standard deviation was 2.536 kg, Diet A appears to be more effective. Participants on Diet A lost more weight on average and showed slightly less variation in outcomes than those on Diet B. This suggests that Diet A not only led to greater weight loss but also produced more consistent results across individuals.
