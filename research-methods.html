About Research Methods and Professional Practice

The module is a structured academic course designed to equip me with essential research skills, critical thinking abilities, and methodological expertise, all of which are crucial for successfully undertaking my dissertation. This module follows a linear structure, meaning that progress is built systematically, with each stage contributing directly to my ability to conduct independent academic research. Unlike other modules, this one emphasizes individual contributions over teamwork in summative assessments, ensuring that I take full ownership of my research development. However, collaborative discussions in online forums provide an opportunity to engage with peers, exchange insights, and refine my understanding through academic discourse.

The tutor-led seminars are a key component of this module, designed to provide in-depth guidance on the scientific investigation process, research ethics, literature review methodologies, and the development of a research proposal. These sessions will help me build a strong foundation in evaluating academic sources, formulating clear and effective research questions, and understanding the ethical considerations involved in conducting research. By the end of the module, I aim to be confident in designing, conducting, and presenting academic research that meets rigorous scholarly standards, ultimately preparing me for the challenges of my dissertation and beyond.
